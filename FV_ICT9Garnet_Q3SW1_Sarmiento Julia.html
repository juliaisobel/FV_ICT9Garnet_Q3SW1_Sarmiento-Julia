9-1-1 TV SHOW

SUMMARY:

9-1-1 is a TV show that shows the struggles and experiences of first responders. In this TV show, we see the life of police officers, firefighters and dispatchers. 9-1-1 also shows various emergencies and crimes that can happen anywhere. This show will let you see, witness and experience intense and exciting stories, events and situations.

CHARACTERS:

ATHENA GRANT

Athena Grant is played by Angela Bassett who won multiple awards in her portrayal as Queen Ramonda in the movie "Black Panther: Wakanda Forever". Athena Grant is one of the famous characters in the show. She is a sergeant in Los Angeles Police Department and she has a very fierce, strong, brave and powerful character.

BOBBY NASH

Bobby Nash is played by Peter Krause. Bobby Nash is also one of the famous and important characters in the show. He is the Captain of Station 118 of the Los Angeles Fire Department. He has a caring, calm personality and he is also a responsible leader and captain.

HEN WILSON

Henrietta Wilson is played by Aisha Hinds. Hen Wilson is a member of the Station 118 of the Los Angeles Fire Department. She is part of the medic team of the fire department and is an aspiring doctor. She also has a jolly and nice personality.
